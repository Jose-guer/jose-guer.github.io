ShockTubes are commonly used in research to generate high enthalpy flows, and high-temperature and pressure enviornments in which to conduct chemical kinetics experiments, or validate optical sensors. The MATLAB code provided here, implements a method of characterisitics solver in MATLAB that can be used to design shocktube to achieve a certain range of target conditions, or to predicit post-incident and post-reflected shock states and test time duration.

The code is available on GitHub.
